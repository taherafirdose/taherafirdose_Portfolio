100 days of Machine Learning

April 14, 2023

Overview: “*100 Days of Machine Learning” is my ambitious journey, diligently chronicled on GitHub, where I committed myself to mastering machine learning. Over this period, I delved deeply into a diverse set of tools and techniques, enhancing both my theoretical understanding and practical skills.

Objective: The aim was to solidify my foundation in machine learning, with a focus on acquiring hands-on experience in various key aspects of the field. This project was a personal challenge to evolve from a learner to a proficient practitioner in machine learning.

Core Learning Areas:

Programming and Data Handling: Gained proficiency in Python and its pivotal libraries like Pandas and NumPy, essential for data manipulation and analysis. Database Management: Enhanced skills in SQL for efficient data querying and management. Data Visualization: Developed competence in creating insightful Tableau Dashboards. Machine Learning Techniques: Explored and implemented core machine learning methodologies including Regression, Classification, and Ensemble Decision Trees, among others. Methodology: I structured the project as a daily log, systematically exploring each area. It included a mix of theoretical learning, coding exercises, and the development of mini-projects to apply the concepts in practical scenarios. This blend ensured a balanced approach, fostering a deeper understanding of both theory and application.

Outcome: “100 Days of Machine Learning” is not just a testament to my dedication and discipline in the realm of AI but also serves as a valuable resource for other learners in the community. It demonstrates my ability to commit to a rigorous learning plan, adaptively overcome challenges, and continuously evolve my skills.

Future Prospects: This project lays the groundwork for my further exploration in the field of machine learning. It forms a solid base from which I can dive into more complex and specialized areas of AI, contributing to innovative solutions in the tech world.
